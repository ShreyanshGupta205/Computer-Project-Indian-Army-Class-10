FAQ & Insights

Everything you want to know about the Indian Armed Forces

Who is the No. 1 special force in India?

MARCOS (Marine Commandos) is considered India's most lethal special force. Though they are trained to engage in battle on all terrains, MARCOS commandos are super-experts in maritime warfare.

How many types of Indian Army services are there?

The Indian Armed Forces consist of three professional uniformed services: the Indian Army (Land), Indian Navy (Sea), and Indian Air Force (Air).

Which states contribute the most to the Indian Army?

With over 1,67,557 soldiers, Uttar Pradesh tops the list of states contributing manpower to the Army, followed by states like Punjab, Haryana, and Rajasthan.

What makes the Indian Army one of the best?

Indian soldiers are considered among the best in the world for high-altitude warfare, jungle combat, and mountain operations. The Siachen conflict is a prime example of their unmatched capability in extreme conditions.

Who is the highest decorated soldier in modern times?

Captain Vikram Batra (Param Vir Chakra) is one of the most celebrated heroes, known for his bravery during the Kargil War of 1999.

Career in Army

For a Permanent commission, you can join via the National Defence Academy (NDA) after class 12th or the Indian Military Academy (IMA) after graduation. Clear the UPSC exam and the SSB interview to begin your journey.
