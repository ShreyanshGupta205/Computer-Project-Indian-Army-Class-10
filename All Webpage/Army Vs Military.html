Army vs Military

Understanding the distinction

The Core Difference

The main difference between a military and an army is that the military is the combined force involving land, air, and navy forces, whereas the army is the group of people who are limited to the land.

An Army also includes forces like paramilitary and field army which are often excluded from a broader military definition in some contexts.
