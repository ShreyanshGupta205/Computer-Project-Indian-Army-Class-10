The Legacy of Valour

A journey through the centuries of military excellence.

The Indian Army is one of the oldest and most respected institutions in India. From the ancient warriors of the Maurya and Maratha empires to the sophisticated modern force of today, our soldiers have always stood for courage and sacrifice.

Our history is marked by massive victories like the 1971 Liberation War and indomitable courage shown in the heights of Kargil. We invite you to explore the detailed chronicles of our brave-hearts in both English and Hindi.
